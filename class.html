CLASS SYSTEM

Choose your path and master your unique abilities. Each class offers distinct gameplay styles and powerful skills.

Available Classes

⚔️ Warrior

Master of melee combat with incredible strength and defense. Lead the charge into battle with unparalleled durability and close-range devastation.

🔥 Mage

Control powerful magic spells to defeat enemies from afar. Command the elements and unleash devastating area-of-effect abilities.

🏹 Archer

Swift and deadly with precise ranged attacks. Strike from any distance with high attack speed and critical damage potential.

🗡️ Assassin

Strike from the shadows with deadly precision and speed. Master stealth and burst damage to eliminate targets swiftly.

⭐ Soulmaster

Master of ancient powers combining magic and combat. Ultimate power unleashed with balanced offense and support abilities.
